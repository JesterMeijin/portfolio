Willo's tale

PreviousNext

Description

Following a violent storm on board his makeshift boat, the little Willo is waking up completely lost on a mystical island seen a little earlier. After recovering his mind, our little explorer sets off on an adventure to find what he came for...

Willo's Tale is a low-poly puzzle and platform game in 3D made with Unity for a school project at Université du Québec à Chicoutimi (UQAC). The principal constraint of this project was to apply and follow the Agile process framework named SCRUM to organize and manage the team's work.

The main mechanic of this game is Willo's ability to take control of his soul by extracting it from his body. With it, the player is able to interact in the Soul World, a mysterious environment where the world we know seems a little changed. By combining the interaction of both worlds, it is possible to solve the first two puzzles of this game prototype.

For my side of the collaboration on this project, I worked especially on the SOLID principles and the Raycasting to be able to create and add interactions in both world. I aslo contributed in the Level Design of the island, made all the menus and the diffenrent parts of the HUD.
