Description

World of Battleship is a battleship game made by three students from Polytech Nantes. This game was first created for a school project with the following theme : Optimisation, choice, random, constraints. To answer this theme, we have created this game with differents Artificial Intelligences to play against players or just to see AI vs AI matches.

My part of the project was mainly focused on the creation of all user interfaces while my partners worked on the development of different AI strategies that we thought of. We used probability combined with randomness to build fairly solid AIs that will be difficult for beginners to overcome.
