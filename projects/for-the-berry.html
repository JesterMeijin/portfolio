For The Berry

Description

In a colorful kingdom where humans and fantastic creatures mingle, the inhabitants lead a happy and peaceful life. Strawberries are the main wealth of this kingdom, which inevitably attracts a lot of lusts ... One day, without anyone noticing, all the strawberries in the kingdom are stolen by malicious greedy and power-hungry creatures. Faced with this unforgivable crime, the ravishing and intrepid princesses of the kingdom are seized of the matter and set off to reconquer the riches of their kingdom!

For the Berry is a local multiplayer game intended to be played with friends on a couch to create collaboration with the right amount of fun and humour ! The main mechanic of this game is the cooperation between players. With the right choices throught a variety of weapons and items and a well prepared group strategy, the communication will be the key to defeat ennemies and boss !

For my side of the collaboration on this project, I worked especially on the GUI with the implementation of the Main Menu and the In-Game Menu. I also greatly contributed in the Level Design of the game and the loading between scenes. Finally, I created the identity of the game : the logo.
